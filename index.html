Steps to running a pure water business in Nigeria

There are different processes involved in starting water business in Nigeria.Below are the steps.


Business Plan And Feasibility Study


When starting a business,the first thing you do is have a clear and well defined plan and create a road mapon how best to go.This is what a bussiness plan does for you.A business plan is a binding document to have at the initial stage of business.Preparing a well defined business plan will help you gather useful and relevant information,gain insights,and drive you to conduct a feasibility study.The resul of your research will form the foundation of your business and help you make informed decisions.also, a business plan plan states the aims and objectives of the business and strategies of how they can be achieved.It involves market research,and analysis,cost analysis,pricing structure,risk analysis,and future forecast.Doing all of these will be easy to source funding from investors.As your business grows,you can tweak your plan because the business plan is a breathing document that can be modified.

secure funding


In the effective management and running of any business,capital is important.The bottled or sachet water business is capital intensive depending on the scale rate you want to operate.There are different cost element you have to consider;the cost of land,erecting of building,borehole drilling,vans,distilling machine,tanks,and other expensive equipment needed for business operation.If you want to run this business as a sole proprietor,you must have enough savings or assets to convert to cash.Other ways to raise money will be to sek out softs loans from family and friends.Also,you can source for partners or investors to work with you. You can as well take out banks loans or apply for availablle grants.


Build a factory



To build a factory,you need land that is situated in a location where your bottled and sachet water will sell.You can lease or build your factory.The building can be big or small but ensure to follow the standard factory layout for commendation/approval of from NAFDAC for the overall ease of production.The layout include a general office,water production room,storage material room,toilets,cloakroom and laboratory.also ensure the production area is free from air pollution.You have to ensure that there is availability of constant water at the factory.Also ensure the factory is situated in a secure area with a good road network for easy accessibility to customers.Futhermore in building your factory you should drill a borehole,carryout a subsurface water analysis before establishing ypour factory as this can save cost during water treatment process.After all of these is achieved you must make a plan for the periodical water treatment.


water purification systems.


The purification system is used to make water fit for drinking.In a bottle or sachet water business,the water purification systems used are;


reverse osmosis



Reverse osmosis(RO)is a water purification process that has gained popularity in recent years due to its ability to remove impurities and provide clean,safe and drinkable water that is 99.9% pure.Reverse osmosis is one of the most effective water purifier there is worldwide.With the use of reverse osmosis be rest assure of making a lot of profit in your water business.


Treatment modules with chemical contents



Treatment modules is one of the relevant water purifying equipment which is being used in the treatment of water.The chemicals which are inside the modules helps in the proper treatment of water.The chemicals helps to reduce the action of bacteria,softens water and also removes odour,colour,taste from water.

Ultraviolet water sterilizer.



Ultraviolet water sterilizer is a purely physical,chemical-free type of water purification.It is a safe and effective method used in residential and industrial water systems.Although the UV not enot for drinking water purification,it is often combined with reverse osmosis systems to provide complete purification.It is the final stage of water treatment/purification.

Other equipments used in the treatment of water are ozones for killing germs in the water,complete filter catridge for the trapping of sediments of different sizes.


Other requirements
